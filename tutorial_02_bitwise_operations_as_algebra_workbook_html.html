Operations at a glance
  • XOR — 1 when bits differ
  • AND — 1 only if both bits are 1
  • OR — 1 if at least one bit is 1
  • NOT — flip every bit (width-dependent)
  • Shift — move bits, fill with 0, edge bits lost
  • Rotate — move bits, wrap edge bits around
Reversibility
  • XOR with known key — ✓ reversible
  • NOT — ✓ reversible
  • Rotation — ✓ reversible
  • Logical shift — ✗ bits may be lost
  • AND mask — ✗ cleared bits gone
  • OR mask — ✗ forced bits hide originals
