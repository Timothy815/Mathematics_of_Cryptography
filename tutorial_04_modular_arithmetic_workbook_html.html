Vocabulary

SymbolMeaning
\(n\)The modulus (wraparound size)
\(a \bmod n\)Remainder of \(a \div n\)
\(\equiv\)Congruence (same remainder)
\(\mathbb{Z}_n\)Residues \(\{0,1,\ldots,n{-}1\}\)

Division Form

Write \(a = qn + r\) where \(0 \le r < n\).

Then \(a \bmod n = r\).

Congruence tests:

\(a \equiv b \pmod n\) iff their remainders match, OR iff \(n \mid (a-b)\).

Arithmetic Rules

Add: \((a+b) \bmod n\) — add, then reduce.

Subtract: \((a-b) \bmod n\) — if result is negative, add \(n\).

Multiply: \((a \cdot b) \bmod n\) — multiply, then reduce.

Caesar Cipher

\(A=0,\;B=1,\;\ldots,\;Z=25\)

Encrypt: \(C \equiv P + K \pmod{26}\)

Decrypt: \(P \equiv C - K \pmod{26}\)

(Add 26 if result is negative.)
