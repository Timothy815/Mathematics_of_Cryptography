Multiplication in \(\mathrm{GF}(2^8)\)

Four drills: xtime no-reduction · xtime with reduction · multiply by 0x03 · full GF(2⁸) multiplication

Drill 1 Drill 2 Drill 3 Drill 4 🏠 Dashboard
Quick Reference — tap to expand
xtime — no reduction Top bit = 0 → just left shift: \(\operatorname{xtime}(a)=a\ll1\)
Example: \(\operatorname{xtime}(\texttt{0x57})=\texttt{0xAE}\)
xtime — with reduction Top bit = 1 → left shift, keep low byte, XOR 0x1B:
\(\operatorname{xtime}(a)=(a\ll1)\oplus\texttt{0x1B}\)
Example: \(\operatorname{xtime}(\texttt{0x83})=\texttt{0x1D}\)
Multiply by 0x03 \(\texttt{0x03}=x+1\)
\(a\cdot\texttt{0x03}=\operatorname{xtime}(a)\oplus a\)
General GF(2⁸) multiply Build xtime chain: \(a,\,xa,\,x^2a,\ldots\)
For each bit \(i\) in \(b\): XOR in \(x^i a\)
AES modulus: 0x11B, fold: 0x1B
